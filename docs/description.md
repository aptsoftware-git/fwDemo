# Military Equipment Readiness & Sustainability: Column Definitions

This document provides a comprehensive breakdown of the standardized reporting columns used for tracking the readiness of various military equipment categories, including Vehicles, Armament, Small Arms, Instruments, and CBRN gear.

---

## 1. B & C Vehicles (Transport & Engineering)
*Tracks soft-skinned transport vehicles (B-Veh) and heavy engineering plant equipment (C-Veh).*

| Column Name | Full Detail | Military Domain Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ser No** | Serial Number | The sequential order of the entries in the report. |
| **Category** | Make & Type | Specifies the manufacturer and specific model/capacity (e.g., 2.5 Ton Truck or Dozer). |
| **Auth (UE)** | Authorized (Unit Establishment) | The official number of units the formation is permitted to have by regulation. |
| **Held (UH)** | Held (Unit Holding) | The actual number of units currently present in the unit's inventory. |
| **MUA** | Minor Under Activity | Units undergoing light, routine maintenance that can be finished quickly at the unit level. |
| **OH** | Overhaul | Units undergoing major strip-down repairs or lifecycle reconditioning. |
| **R4** | Repair Level 4 | Heavy repairs that must be sent to specialized field or base workshops. |
| **Total NMC** | Total Non-Mission Capable | The sum of all unserviceable units (MUA + OH + R4). |
| **FMC** | Fully Mission Capable | Units that are 100% fit for immediate operational deployment. |
| **Remarks** | VOR / EOA Details | Notes on **Vehicle Off Road (VOR)** demands or **Equipment On Account (EOA)** status. |

---

## 2. Armament & Small Arms (Weaponry)
*Covers artillery, mortars, and individual/crew-served firearms.*

| Column Name | Full Detail | Military Domain Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Make & Eqpt** | Nomenclature | The specific model and name of the weapon system (e.g., 155mm Gun or 5.56mm Rifle). |
| **Eng/Brl** | Engineering / Barrel | Failures specifically related to barrel wear-out or major structural engineering faults. |
| **Spares** | Lack of Spares | Equipment grounded specifically because replacement parts are currently unavailable. |
| **OH/MR/FR/R4** | Repair Pipeline | Status of weapons undergoing Overhaul, Medium, Field, or Level 4 repairs. |
| **OBE** | Out of Bounds | Equipment designated as Beyond Economic Repair and likely to be scrapped. |
| **PMC (Nos)** | Partially Mission Capable | Weapons that are functional but have minor defects that don't prevent use. |
| **NMC %** | Non-Mission Capable % | The percentage of total holdings that are currently unserviceable. |
| **FMC %** | Fully Mission Capable % | The primary readiness metric; the percentage of arms ready for immediate combat. |
| **Avl %** | Availability % | The total percentage of equipment (FMC + PMC) available for use. |

---

## 3. Instruments (Optical & Electronic)
*Tracks sensitive gear like night vision devices, rangefinders, and surveying tools.*

| Column Name | Full Detail | Military Domain Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NMC due to: Spares** | Non-Mission Capable (Spares) | Instruments waiting for specific optical or electronic components. |
| **Under OH /R4/FR** | Specialized Repair | Instruments undergoing high-level technical calibration or repair in workshops. |
| **Present loc of eqpt** | Physical Location | Specifies which workshop or sub-unit currently has physical custody of the item. |

---

## 4. CBRN Equipment (Non-Conventional Defense)
*Tracks equipment for Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear defense.*

| Column Name | Full Detail | Military Domain Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **DRWO** | Workshop Order | Equipment sent to a workshop with an active District/Divisional Repair Workshop Order. |
| **DR Raised** | Demand Raised | A formal request for a replacement or a repair job has been initiated. |
| **‘S’** | Serviceable | The total quantity of CBRN gear fit for immediate operational use. |
| **UNSV** | Unserviceable | The total count of items that are currently broken or non-functional. |

---
**Key Abbreviations:**
* **FMN**: Formation
* **EME**: Electronics and Mechanical Engineers
* **EOA**: Equipment On Account
* **VOR**: Vehicle Off Road
